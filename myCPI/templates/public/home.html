Welcome to MyCPI

The Consumer Price Index (CPI) is a measure of the average change over time in the prices paid by urban consumers for a fixed market basket of consumer goods and services. The CPI reflects the spending pattern of all urban consumers.

MyCPI is an individualized measure of price change. MyCPI allows you to track the prices of a market basket that more closely reflects your spending habits and to compare your CPI to the national average.
